ABOUT THE CLUB

Technical interviewing is an important skill that encompasses technical knowledge, problem solving, and communication. Tech companies like Google, Microsoft, Twitter, and countless other companies often ask their internship and full-time candidates to solve technical questions using Data Structures and Algorithms as a means of evaluating skills.

Technical interviews can be daunting, but there's a science to it! We can help demystify the process and provide great practice. Come to The Coding Interview Club where you can practice technical problems from sites like Leetcode, get advice from students who have previously gone through the interview process, and attend mock interviews run by the club.

"Every great developer you know got there by solving problems they were unqualified to solve until they actually did it."

--Patrick McKenzie
